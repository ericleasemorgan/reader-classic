URL Carrel

Use this form to queue the creation of a study carrel based on the content of a URL.

Given a URL, the Reader will harvest/cache the linked content. The Reader will then extract any URLs it finds in the content and cache that content as well. The Reader will then stop caching and begin processing.

This is a good proceedure to: 1) get an overview of a website, read a blog, or read the whole of a Wikipedia page and its citations, 2) garner a list of additional URLs for further processing.
