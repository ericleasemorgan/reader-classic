The Distant Reader Library

This is the fledgling home page of the Distant Reader Library, a curated collection of "study carrels". As of right now, there is only one thing here, the library itself. Please feel free to:

  1. browse the collection
  2. peruse any of the carrels
  3. download any of the carrels for offline reading & analysis
  4. use the functions contained in the Distant Reader Toolbox to increase your understanding

Thank you for using the Distant Reader, and don't hesitate to tell your friends.
